# Bokii Cool Solutions Logo Brief

## Recommended Concept

A bold circular badge logo that combines a snowflake and a wrench, with a subtle motion line to communicate mobile service.

## Why This Works

- Snowflake immediately communicates cooling and refrigeration
- Wrench communicates repair and technical skill
- Circular badge shape works well on trailers, uniforms, and profile images
- Motion line or road element adds the mobile service angle

## Logo Style

- Clean
- Bold
- High contrast
- Easy to read from a distance
- Suitable for signage and stickers

## Recommended Text Lockup

### Main Name

`Bokii Cool Solutions`

### Supporting Line

`Mobile Fridge, Aircon & Refrigeration Repair`

## Preferred Colors

- Ice Blue
- Navy
- White
- Charcoal

Use orange only as a highlight if needed.

## What to Avoid

- Overly complicated icons
- Thin lines that disappear on trailer branding
- Soft pastel colors
- Generic house or abstract swoosh logos
- Script fonts that reduce legibility
