# Bokii Cool Solutions

## Brand Position

Bokii Cool Solutions is a mobile refrigeration and cooling repair business focused on fast, reliable, practical service. The brand should feel trustworthy, skilled, and ready to come to the customer.

## Core Services

- Mobile fridge repair
- Aircon repair
- Refrigerator repair
- Cold room and commercial cooler support
- Mobile cooling trailer service and maintenance
- Fault finding and regassing

## Brand Personality

- Reliable
- Skilled
- Fast to respond
- Hardworking
- Professional without feeling expensive
- Local and approachable

## Brand Direction

The best direction is a bold service brand built around cooling, motion, and technical skill.

Use a look that combines:

- Cool blues to represent refrigeration and air conditioning
- Dark charcoal or black for strength and trust
- A bright accent like orange or lime for energy and visibility on vehicles and signage

## Recommended Color Palette

- Ice Blue: `#1FA7E1`
- Deep Navy: `#103A5E`
- Charcoal: `#1F252B`
- Frost White: `#F4F9FC`
- Signal Orange: `#FF7A00`

## Logo Direction

### Primary Concept

Create a strong emblem using:

- A snowflake or cooling swirl
- A wrench or spanner
- A subtle van, trailer, or motion line to show mobile service

The logo should feel simple enough for:

- Trailer branding
- Work shirts
- WhatsApp profile images
- Business cards
- Invoices and quotations
- Social media

### Suggested Layout

Use a stacked or badge-style mark:

**Icon**
Snowflake merged with a wrench inside a circle or shield.

**Wordmark**
`Bokii Cool Solutions`

**Tagline**
`Mobile Fridge, Aircon & Refrigeration Repair`

## Suggested Fonts

Use bold, clean, industrial-looking fonts:

- Oswald
- Bebas Neue
- Montserrat SemiBold
- Exo 2

For a strong logo and signage system, pair:

- Headings: `Oswald` or `Bebas Neue`
- Body text: `Montserrat`

## Photo Style Guidance

Your existing photos show real work, real tools, and real field service. That is valuable because it builds trust. When used in marketing:

- Prefer real job photos over generic stock images
- Crop tighter around tools, gauges, trailers, and repair action
- Use branded blue overlays for consistency
- Add before-and-after stories where possible

## Brand Message

Customers should immediately understand three things:

1. You repair cooling systems.
2. You come to them.
3. You are practical and dependable.
